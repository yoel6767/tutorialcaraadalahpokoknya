Description[]

Fiend is one of the jungle buff monsters. It is located on the right side of the map almost on the bottom lane near the second turret. After killing it, it gives moderate gold than its purple counterparts but will also give an Orange Buff (also known as Red Buff.)

Story[]

  • Sanctum Island
  • Imperial Sanctuary

Molten Fiend was formed by the residual consciousness of an ancient Evil God. With its whole body engulfed in Primal Lightning, Molten Fiend will launch ruthless attacks on anyone who dares step into its territory.

There are a lot of creatures in the Imperial Sanctuary. Fiend, being the most aggressive creatures among them, has been resting beside the Temple of Heroes. Its powerful strength is its way of protecting itself. Moreover, the most appalling thing about it is that it possesses the power of genetic mutation so that it could adjust its strength according to the ability of its components. It is said that the warriors could regain the gene of its strength after defeating it.

Appearance[]

Molten Fiend is a monster with appearance to be improvisation of wolf. This creature is named Fiend in Imperial Sanctuary map and is also known as Core Guard in Celestial Palace and Western Expanse maps.

Fiend
Core Guard

Tips[]

Fiend has a spawn time of 45 seconds and a further cooldown of 90 seconds. Fiend is comparatively, easy to kill, unlike Turtle and you must kill the Fiend, as soon as you see one. By killing a Fiend, you not only obtain gold and experience but you also get a buff that boosts your physical and magical damage by 10%. So, it is advised, you should never miss the opportunity to score some gold, experience, and buffs, from the Fiend.

History[]

The oldest creep that taken this position was Reaper.





Patch Notes 1.6.42#IV. Battlefield Adjustments

  • Orange Buff: The killer will also gain extra Penetration (10% for Marksman, Mage, and Support and 5% for Fighter, Tank, and Assassin. Gains Physical Penetration if the hero's extra Physical Attack isn't lower than their extra Magic Power, and Magic Penetration if otherwise) for the duration.

Patch Notes 1.5.38

We observed that, due to the duration and spawn intervals of buffs, Junglers always found themselves idle when jungling. Besides, if the side at a disadvantage loses buffs, their situation will worsen for a long time. Therefore, we're trying to make proper adjustments to the duration and spawn intervals of buffs. We will pay close attention to the effect of these adjustments and consider possible updates accordingly.

  • Fiend
    • NERF Spawn Interval: 120s 90s
    • BUFF Spawn Time: 30s 25s
    • NERF Physical and Magic Defense Growth per minute: 5 2
    • NERF Exp Growth: 5 2.5
    • NERF Base Gold: 43 32
    • NERF Gold Growth: 1.43 1.07
      • BUFF Duration of Soul of Wind buff and Soul of Lava buff: 120s 75s

Patch Notes 1.4.50

Soul of Lava: Physical Attack Bonus of Marksman/Mage/Support decreased from 100% to 80%

Patch Notes 1.4.36

Core Guard:

A. Basic HP decreased. Basic ATK and Magic Defense increased. The initial refresh time decreased from 33s to 30s.

B. The Physical and Magic Defense scale with game time.

C. Adjusted the buff: Slow enemies down and deal extra True Damage when dealing damage to enemies. The effect cools down for a period of time.

D. When Tank/Assassin/Fighter Heroes get this buff. they will inflict stronger Slowing Effect to enemies but deals less extra damage. When Mage/Marksman/Support Heroes get this buff, they will deal more damage but inflict weaker Slowing Effect.

Patch Notes 1.3.44

Patch Notes 1.3.34

3.Death: Revamped as [Core Guard]. Optimized the display effect of Health Pack.

Patch Notes 1.3.32#III. Battlefield Adjustments

Death : Adjusted HP growth to 2882 from 3843. After being slain it will leave a Health Pack that can regenerate some HP and Mana.
